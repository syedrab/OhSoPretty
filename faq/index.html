FAQ

Your questions, thoughtfully answered.

From technique differences to aftercare, here is everything you need before booking your appointment.

Are nano brows and ombre brows the same?

No. Nano brows create hyper-realistic hair strokes for a natural texture, while ombre brows (powder brows) use soft shading to create a makeup-like gradient. Both are semi-permanent and machine-based, but the finish is different.

How long does an appointment take?

Most sessions take 2 to 3 hours, including consultation, mapping, and the procedure itself.

How long do results last?

Results typically last 1 to 3 years depending on skin type, aftercare, and lifestyle. Nano brows often fade a bit softer than ombre.

What is the healing process like?

Brows soften and fade slightly over a few weeks. We provide detailed aftercare instructions to support healing and pigment retention.

Is there a touch-up?

Yes, touch-up appointments are available and recommended to perfect shape, color, and density.

Who is not a good candidate?

We review medical history, medications, and skin conditions during consultation to make sure the service is safe and appropriate.

How should I prepare?

Avoid retinol, alcohol, and sun exposure before your appointment. You will receive a full prep guide once booked.
