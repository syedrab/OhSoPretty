Ombre Brows (Powder Brows)

A soft, shaded technique that creates a makeup-inspired finish with a lighter front and a deeper, defined tail.

The finish

Polished, feminine, and softly defined.

Ombre brows are created with tiny, layered dots that build a smooth gradient. The look is refined and even, with a gentle fade that mimics softly applied makeup.

Best for

Clients who want a defined brow with long-lasting polish and a soft finish.

Technique

Machine shading builds dimension without harsh lines, creating a seamless fade.

Longevity

Typically 1 to 3 years, with even fading when properly maintained.

Process

A calm, guided experience.

01 Consultation We review your style, desired density, and long-term goals.
02 Mapping Precise shaping ensures balance and harmony with your features.
03 Ombre shading Soft layering creates a smooth gradient that looks polished and refined.
04 Aftercare You leave with detailed guidance for a beautiful healing process.
