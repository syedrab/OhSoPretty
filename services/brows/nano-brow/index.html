Nano Brows

Hyper-realistic hair strokes that mimic the natural texture of brow hairs for a soft, realistic finish.

The finish

Natural texture with subtle definition.

Nano brows use a machine to create fine, hair-like strokes. The result is a textured, natural look that enhances your shape without a makeup effect.

Best for

Clients who want realistic texture and a natural, airy finish.

Technique

Machine-based strokes mimic individual hairs for a soft, dimensional result.

Longevity

Typically 1 to 2 years, with subtle, natural fading over time.

Process

A calm, guided experience.

01 Consultation We review your goals, desired density, and daily routine.
02 Mapping Precise shaping creates balance and harmony with your features.
03 Nano strokes Fine, hair-like strokes are layered to create soft definition and texture.
04 Aftercare You leave with detailed guidance for a beautiful healing process.
